My special rice recipe

Advisor Ndane Ndazhaga

Here's a sure way to prepare a delicious rice. Measure two cups of rice, Put a jug of water on the fire
and allow to boil add your rice into the water and let it cook till the water is soaked up in the rice.
Bring down the rice and wash with cold water. This is to remove the starch from the rice.
Boil some water and add salt to taste. Pour the rice in to the salted water ensure the water level is same
as the quantity of rice. Cook till the water dries off from the rice.
The rice is ready to eat. Serve and eat with and soup or stew of your choice.
